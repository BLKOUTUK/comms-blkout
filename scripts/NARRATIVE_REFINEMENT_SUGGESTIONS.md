## Goal
Make the argument breathe, add humor, ensure punchlines land with impact

- Add "Yeah" for conversational rhythm
- Feels more like mutual acknowledgment

**Card 3** (Sharpen the question):

- Italicize "knew" for emphasis
- Slightly more pointed

**NEW Card 4.5** (Add humor after poll):

## PACING PRINCIPLES

1. **Heavy → Light → Heavy rhythm**
   - Don't stack too many serious cards
   - Add breathing cards between big insights
   - Use humor to give audience permission to feel

2. **Setup → Punchline structure**
   - Big insights need 1-2 setup cards
   - Don't drop revelations cold
   - Let punchlines sit alone in larger cards

3. **Conversational tone**
   - "You know that moment when..."
   - "Yeah, we're really saying..."
   - Acknowledge audience skepticism with humor

4. **Card sizing for emphasis**
   - Small cards: Quick hits, connective tissue
   - Medium cards: Standard statements
   - Large cards: Key insights, interactions
   - Hero cards: THE BIG MOMENTS (Inversion, thesis, invitation)
