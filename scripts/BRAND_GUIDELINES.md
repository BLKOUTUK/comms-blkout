### Logo Placement Rules
**ALWAYS include official logo in EVERY graphic**
- Position: Bottom right corner (preferred) OR top center
- Size: Minimum 80px height, clearly visible but not dominating
- Clear space: Maintain padding of at least 20px from edges
- Background: Ensure sufficient contrast for legibility

### ‚ùå Logo Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort logo
- Don't change logo colors
- Don't add effects (drop shadow, glow, etc.)
- Don't rotate logo
- Don't place logo over busy backgrounds without contrast treatment

## üîó Call-to-Action (CTA) Requirements

**ALWAYS include clear next step, even when obvious**

### CTA Format
```
[Action Verb] + [Destination]
```

### Approved CTAs by Context

**Newsletter Subscription:**
- ‚úÖ "Subscribe: BLKOUTUK.COM/newsletter"
- ‚úÖ "Get updates: BLKOUTUK.COM/newsletter"
- ‚úÖ "Join newsletter: Link in bio"

**General Information:**
- ‚úÖ "Learn more: BLKOUTUK.COM"
- ‚úÖ "Visit: BLKOUTUK.COM"
- ‚úÖ "Explore: BLKOUTUK.COM"

**Events:**
- ‚úÖ "Register: BLKOUTHUB.COM/events/[event-id]"
- ‚úÖ "RSVP: Link in bio"
- ‚úÖ "Join us: BLKOUTUK.COM/events"

**Membership:**
- ‚úÖ "Become a member: BLKOUTUK.COM"
- ‚úÖ "Join: BLKOUTUK.COM/join"

**Apps:**
- ‚úÖ "Download: BLKOUTUK.COM/apps"
- ‚úÖ "Get early access: Subscribe at BLKOUTUK.COM"

### CTA Placement
- Position: Bottom of graphic, above logo
- Background: Contrasting box (gold #f59e0b for emphasis, purple #a855f7 for secondary)
- Font: Bold, minimum 24-32pt depending on graphic size
- Color: High contrast text (white on dark, black on light)

### ‚ùå CTA Don'ts
- Never assume the user knows what to do
- Don't use vague CTAs like "Click here" or "Find out more"
- Don't omit the URL/destination
- Don't make CTAs too small to read on mobile

## üé® Brand Colors - CORRECTED HARMONIOUS PALETTE

### ‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Previous palette (Purple #a855f7, Gold #f59e0b, Green #10b981, Teal #14b8a6) did not work as complementary colors and has been replaced.

### PRIMARY: Purple Family Spectrum (Indigo ‚Üí Electric Pink)
**Use for backgrounds, headings, gradients, and main brand elements**

```
Indigo (cool purple):    indigo-* (e.g., indigo-950, indigo-400)
Purple (core brand):     purple-* (e.g., purple-950, purple-400)
Violet (warm purple):    violet-* (e.g., violet-950, violet-400)
Fuchsia (purple-pink):   fuchsia-* (e.g., fuchsia-500, fuchsia-400)
Pink (electric pink):    pink-* (e.g., pink-500, pink-400)
```

**Gradient Examples (recognizably BLKOUT at a glance):**
- Hero backgrounds: `from-indigo-950 via-purple-950 to-violet-950`
- Headings: `from-indigo-400 via-purple-400 to-fuchsia-400`
- Dynamic text: `from-violet-400 via-fuchsia-400 to-pink-400`
- Buttons: `from-indigo-600 to-fuchsia-600`

### ACCENTS: Gold, Teal, Emerald (Use Sparingly)
**Use for highlights, secondary CTAs, and visual interest**

```
Gold:       amber-* (e.g., amber-400, amber-600)
Teal:       teal-* (e.g., teal-400, teal-500)
Emerald:    emerald-* (e.g., emerald-400, emerald-500)
```

**Accent Usage:**
- Partnership/community elements: amber-300, teal-300, emerald-300
- Secondary CTAs: Border with amber-400, hover to amber background
- Icon highlights: Use accent colors on specific icons for visual interest

### Design Voice Principles
**"Recognizably BLKOUT at a glance"** means:
1. **Purple dominance**: 70-80% purple family spectrum
2. **Accent highlights**: 20-30% gold/teal/emerald accents for contrast
3. **Font-black headings**: Bold, uppercase, high impact
4. **Font-light body text**: Readable, elegant, approachable
5. **Gradient depth**: Deep backgrounds (950), bright text (400-500)
- **Green (#10b981)**: Community, health, positive metrics
- **Teal (#14b8a6)**: Innovation, technology, secondary accents

### Gradients
Approved gradient combinations:
- Purple ‚Üí Gold ‚Üí Green (festive, celebratory)
- Purple ‚Üí Teal (tech-forward, modern)
- Gold ‚Üí Green (warm, community-focused)

### Accessibility
- Ensure text has minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio against backgrounds
- Use white text on dark colors, dark text on light colors
- Test legibility at mobile sizes

## üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Missing logo** - Never ship a graphic without BLKOUT official logo
2. **Vague CTAs** - Always tell people exactly what to do next with URL
3. **App confusion** - Critical Frequency = mental health, Down = hook-up
4. **Over-complication** - Keep technical concepts simple and visual
5. **Missing URLs** - Always include BLKOUTUK.COM or specific landing page
6. **Poor contrast** - Text must be readable on all backgrounds
7. **Inconsistent branding** - Stick to approved colors and logo usage
