# BLKOUT Board Recruitment - Canva Design Brief

## Campaign: Join Our Board
## Format: Instagram Carousel (6 slides, 1080x1080 or 1080x1350)

---

## Brand Guidelines

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Primary Purple** | #7C3AED |
| **Gold Accent** | #F59E0B |
| **Deep Black** | #0F0F0F |
| **Off-White** | #FAFAFA |
| **Fonts** | Use existing BLKOUT brand fonts |

**Visual Style:** Bold, liberation-focused, community-centered

---

## Slide 1: Hook / Cover

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll, create intrigue

**Layout:**
- Full background: Deep purple gradient or BLKOUT branded background
- Center-aligned text

**Text Content:**
```
[Top - Small]
BLKOUT IS RECRUITING

[Center - Large/Bold]
JOIN OUR
BOARD

[Bottom - Medium]
5 positions. Your community. Our future.
```

**Visual Elements:**
- BLKOUT logo (top or bottom corner)
- Optional: Subtle pattern or texture from brand kit

---

## Slide 2: The Opportunity

**Purpose:** Explain what this is

**Layout:**
- Left-aligned or center text
- Clean, readable

**Text Content:**
```
[Header]
We're building something extraordinary.

[Body]
BLKOUT is now a registered
Community Benefit Society.

We need leaders to guide us home.

[Footer]
10 years of community.
Now it's time for governance.
```

**Visual Elements:**
- Community Benefit Society badge/icon (optional)
- Gold accent line or element

---

## Slide 3: The Positions

**Purpose:** Show the 5 board roles

**Layout:**
- Grid or list format
- Each position with icon

**Text Content:**
```
[Header]
5 POSITIONS AVAILABLE

[Grid/List]
🪑 Chair
   Lead our governance with vision

💷 Treasurer
   Steward our community resources

📋 Secretary
   Keep our house in order

💻 Technology Director
   Guide our digital future

🤝 Community Director
   Amplify grassroots voices
```

**Visual Elements:**
- Icons or emojis for each role
- Purple/gold color blocking

---

## Slide 4: Who We're Looking For

**Purpose:** Make it feel accessible

**Layout:**
- Two columns or stacked sections

**Text Content:**
```
[Header]
WHO WE'RE LOOKING FOR

[Left Column - "You, Beloved"]
✓ Black queer men who care deeply
✓ Bring lived experience (that's enough)
✓ Can commit 4-6 hours monthly
✓ Want to learn and grow with us

[Right Column or Below]
Heart for the work? Essential.
Formal qualifications? Optional.
```

**Visual Elements:**
- Checkmarks or bullet points
- Warm, inviting tone in design

---

## Slide 5: Timeline

**Purpose:** Create urgency, show key dates

**Layout:**
- Timeline graphic or date cards
- Clear visual hierarchy

**Text Content:**
```
[Header]
KEY DATES

[Timeline]
📅 FEB 14
   EOI Deadline

📅 FEB 15-16
   Info Session on BLKOUTHUB

📅 TBA
   Board Elections at AGM
```

**Visual Elements:**
- Calendar icons
- Gold accent on dates
- Arrow or flow connecting dates

---

## Slide 6: Call to Action

**Purpose:** Drive action

**Layout:**
- Center-aligned
- Bold CTA button style

**Text Content:**
```
[Top]
The revolution needs governance, beloved.
And governance needs you.

[Center - Large]
APPLY NOW

[Bottom]
blkoutuk.com/governance

Join or reactivate your
BLKOUTHUB membership first
```

**Visual Elements:**
- Button-style CTA
- QR code to governance page (optional)
- BLKOUT logo
