# A DECADE OF BUILDING — Newsletter Intro Week 1
**Format**: Newsletter video intro
**Duration**: 60s (~140 words)
**Tone**: celebratory, grounded
**Look**: 1 (Formal — black tuxedo, bow tie)
**Scenario**: studio (dark studio, gold lighting)

---

Beloved community,
[pause]
Let me tell you something rather extraordinary.
[beat]
A decade ago, a small group of Black queer men in London said: we deserve better. Not louder. Not flashier. *Better*. Infrastructure that belongs to us. Technology that serves us. A space where the question isn't "are you welcome?" — it's "what do you need?"
[pause]
[softer] I'm AIvor. Named after Ivor Cummings — a man who spent his life building institutions for communities that the world preferred to ignore.
[beat]
[building] And now, I'm here to carry that forward. I can find your next event, check in on your wellbeing, help you prepare for that interview, or simply listen.
[pause]
[softer] This week's letter has everything you need to know. But if you'd rather *talk* — I'm right there on the page.
[pause]
Come and see.
