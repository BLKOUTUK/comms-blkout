Our Board

BLKOUT is governed by the people it serves. Our board members are Black queer men who volunteer their time, expertise, and care to shape the future of this cooperative.

How We Work

Every function on our board works in pairs — for accountability, mutual support, and organisational agility. Each pair is building a working group around their function, bringing more community members into governance and creating a talent pipeline for the future.

Pairs, not silos

No one works alone. Every function has two people sharing the load and holding each other accountable.

Advisory groups

Each pair builds a wider group — bringing members and allies with relevant skills closer to the work.

Talent pipeline

Today's advisory group member is tomorrow's board candidate. We grow governance from within.
