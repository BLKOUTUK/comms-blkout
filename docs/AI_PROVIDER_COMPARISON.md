### Problem

- Gemini might generate one aesthetic
- Wan Video might generate a completely different look
- User sees inconsistent brand identity

### Solution: Validation Layer Enforces Consistency

**New ValuesCheck validation includes:**

1. **Brand Voice Recognizable** (required check)
   - Question: "Content is recognizably BLKOUT at a glance? (colors, typography, style)"
   - Help text: "Purple (#a855f7), Gold (#f59e0b), Green (#10b981), Teal (#14b8a6). IVOR voice: sophisticated wit meets Black British warmth."

2. **Visual Consistency** (required check)
   - Question: "Visual design matches BLKOUT brand guidelines? (regardless of AI provider)"
   - Help text: "Consistent aesthetic whether generated by Gemini, Wan Video, or other providers. Brand voice > provider."
