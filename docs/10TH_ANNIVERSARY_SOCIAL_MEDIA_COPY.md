## LinkedIn Post

### Professional Impact Report
**Timing:** February 10th, 8:00 AM GMT

**Content:**
```
A Decade of Liberation: BLKOUT's 10th Anniversary

Today marks 10 years since BLKOUT was founded - a community organization serving Black queer men in the UK that has evolved into a pioneering example of community-owned technology.

THE JOURNEY (2016-2026)

What began as informal gatherings in London has grown into:
- A digital platform serving 10,000+ community members
- A Community Benefit Society with democratic governance
- A technology stack including AI assistants, event management, and e-commerce
- A model for ethical, community-owned tech development

2025 ACHIEVEMENTS

This past year, we launched:

AI for Liberation
- IVOR AI Assistant: Culturally competent support across WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, and web
- 6 specialized AI agents managing communications (Griot, Listener, Weaver, Strategist, Herald, Concierge)
- Liberation Layer 3: Mathematical enforcement of 75% creator revenue share

Community Infrastructure
- Events Calendar connecting Black queer men across the UK
- News Platform amplifying community voices
- Communications Hub with values-first content generation

Governance Innovation
- Community Benefit Society registration
- Democratic decision-making protocols
- Transparent operations and member ownership

2026 ROADMAP

- Critical Frequency: Social discovery platform (Q1)
- Down: Wellness companion app (Spring)
- BLKOUT Shop: E-commerce with creator marketplace
- Governance Portal: Member voting and participation

THE MODEL

We're demonstrating that:
- Technology can center marginalized communities by design
- Ethical values can be mathematically enforced (not just aspirational)
- Community ownership is viable at scale
- AI can be liberatory, not extractive

Tonight, we celebrate with a hybrid event in London and virtually worldwide. Platform demos, community stories, and visioning the next decade.

For those interested in community-owned technology, cooperative governance, or ethical AI development - let's connect. Building liberation technology is collective work.

#SocialInnovation #CommunityOwnership #EthicalTech #AIForGood #LGBTQInclusion #CooperativeTech #TechForGood #UKTech #CommunityBenefitSociety #LiberationTechnology #10YearsOfBLKOUT
```
