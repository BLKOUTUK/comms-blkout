## ðŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### Email Issues
**Problem:** Low open rate (<20%)
**Solution:**
- Check spam folder placement
- A/B test new subject line
- Send reminder with different subject on Dec 26th

**Problem:** Broken images in email
**Solution:**
- Ensure all images are publicly hosted
- Test with Litmus or Email on Acid
- Have plain-text fallback ready

### Social Media Issues
**Problem:** Low engagement in first 2 hours
**Solution:**
- Share to BLKOUT team group chats for initial boost
- Cross-post to relevant Black queer UK groups
- Pin critical posts

**Problem:** Quiz RSVP count low (<100 by Dec 27th)
**Solution:**
- Send urgent reminder email Dec 27th evening
- Create urgency: "Last 20 spots!" (even if not true)
- Extend capacity if needed

### Event Day Issues
**Problem:** Zoom link not working
**Solution:**
- Have backup Zoom room ready
- Send new link via email + social media
- Start 15 mins late to allow stragglers

**Problem:** Low attendance day-of
**Solution:**
- Send "starting in 30 mins!" text/WhatsApp blast
- Go live on Instagram to pull in viewers
- Record session for those who couldn't make it
